Our Pricing Principles

Why we price this way — not just what it costs.

Flat fees, never interest

No financing structures that accrue interest on unpaid balances. If a payment is late, we talk and renegotiate terms — we don't compound a debt against you, per the Quran's clear prohibition of riba (2:278-279).

No hidden fees, ever

The number on this page is the number you pay. Any third-party cost (ad spend, software licenses) is itemized and billed at cost, never marked up in the dark — that would be gharar, the deception our standards explicitly forbid.

We turn away misaligned budgets

If a business can't sustainably afford a tier, we'll say so honestly rather than upsell beyond their means — financial pressure on a Muslim brother or sister isn't a sale we want to make.

30-day exit, no penalty

You're never locked into a long contract out of fear of an exit fee. If the relationship isn't working for either of us, 30 days notice ends it cleanly — trust shouldn't require a legal cage.
